# People

Primary home for important people who don't fit cleanly into `relationship`, `family`, or `work`.

## What goes here

- Friends, neighbors, mentors, acquaintances.
- People the user keeps mentioning but who aren't a partner, family member, or work contact.
- Per-person notes (use `templates/person-note-template.md`).

## What does NOT go here

- A spouse or partner (use `relationship/`).
- A parent / sibling / child (use `family/`).
- A boss / coworker / client (use `work/`).
