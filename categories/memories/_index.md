# Memories

Primary home for important memories, life events, personal history, stories, and context worth preserving long-term.

## What goes here

- Major life events the user wants ChatGPT to know about.
- Stories that explain who the user is.
- Childhood, family, formative experiences.
- "Don't forget I told you about that" type context.

## What does NOT go here

- Active relationships (use `relationship/`, `family/`, or `people/`).
- Active projects or work (use the relevant category).
