# Family

Primary home for family context: parents, children, siblings, extended family, household dynamics, and caregiving responsibilities.

## What goes here

- Parents, in-laws, siblings, kids, grandparents, extended family.
- Recurring household dynamics that aren't romantic relationship dynamics.
- Caregiving context: aging parents, kids' needs, family health.

## What does NOT go here

- The user's romantic partner (use `relationship/`).
- A friend (use `people/`).
