# Business

Primary home for business ideas, companies, customers, products, operations, monetization, and launch plans.

## What goes here

- The user's own company / startup / side business.
- Customers, leads, pricing, monetization plans.
- Product decisions and operational context.
- Launches and growth experiments.

## What does NOT go here

- A non-revenue personal project (use `projects/`).
- A pure idea (use `ideas/`).
