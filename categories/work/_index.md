# Work

Primary home for job context: career decisions, coworkers, bosses, clients, customers, workplace patterns, meetings, and recurring work topics.

## What goes here

- Job and career decisions.
- Patterns with a boss, manager, coworker, client, or customer.
- Workplace dynamics and recurring tensions.
- Specific meetings, projects, or deliverables tied to the user's job.

## What does NOT go here

- A personal side project (use `projects/`).
- A revenue-bearing business of the user's own (use `business/`).
- A friend who happens to work somewhere (use `people/`).
