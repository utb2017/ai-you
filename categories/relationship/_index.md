# Relationship

Primary home for relationship context: patterns, decisions, conversations, boundaries, emotional context, and recurring partner / spouse topics.

## What goes here

- Long-running relationship dynamics with a partner, spouse, girlfriend, or boyfriend.
- Emotional context that should carry between chats.
- Decisions, agreements, and boundaries inside the relationship.
- Recurring conflict patterns and the user's preferred way of handling them.

## What does NOT go here

- A friend or neighbor (use `people/`).
- A parent / sibling / child (use `family/`).
- A boss / coworker / client (use `work/`).
