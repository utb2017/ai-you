# Ideas

Primary home for ideas, theories, prompts, creative concepts, mental models, and future possibilities.

## What goes here

- Half-formed ideas worth not losing.
- Theories the user keeps coming back to.
- Mental models, frameworks, and recurring intellectual interests.
- Prompts and concepts for future creative or business work.

## What does NOT go here

- An active project the user is building (use `projects/`).
- A revenue-bearing business (use `business/`).
