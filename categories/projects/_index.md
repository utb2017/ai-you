# Projects

Primary home for personal projects: creative work, apps, builds, writing, plans, and ongoing project context.

## What goes here

- Apps, builds, side projects.
- Creative projects: writing, music, video, design.
- Ongoing personal initiatives that aren't a job and aren't a business.

## What does NOT go here

- Work projects tied to an employer (use `work/`).
- A revenue-bearing company (use `business/`).
- A pure idea you haven't started yet (use `ideas/`).
