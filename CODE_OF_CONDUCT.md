# Code of Conduct

AI You is for normal humans trying to make AI a little less forgetful. The goal is calm, respectful collaboration. The rules are short.

## Be decent

- Treat issue threads, PR reviews, and discussions like a conversation with a stranger you respect.
- Disagree with the work, not the person.
- Don't make jokes at the expense of someone's identity, body, mental health, or background.
- Don't dox, threaten, or harass anyone.

## Don't paste personal data into public spaces

This repo is a public template. Issues, PRs, and discussions are public. Don't paste:

- your private repo contents,
- real names of people in your life,
- secrets, credentials, or private health details.

If you need to share an example, invent the data.

## Maintainer authority

Maintainers may edit, lock, or delete content that breaks these rules, and may block contributors who repeatedly violate them. We will try to explain why.

## Reporting

If something is off, open a non-public report through GitHub's [report abuse](https://docs.github.com/en/communities/maintaining-your-safety-on-github/reporting-abuse-or-spam) flow, or email the repo maintainer.

## Inspiration

This document borrows in spirit from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), simplified for a small project.
